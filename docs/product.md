## Product Thinking

## Problem

Businesses have access to data but struggle to turn it into actions.

## Solution

A decision layer on top of Stripe that converts revenue data into clear recommendations.

## Insight

The biggest gap is not data. It is decision-making.

## Why Stripe

Stripe has the cleanest view of revenue and customer behavior, making it the ideal foundation.

## Tradeoffs

Did not build full accounting features to stay focused on revenue decisions

Started with Stripe data only to keep MVP simple and real-time

Prioritized actionable outputs over perfect accuracy
